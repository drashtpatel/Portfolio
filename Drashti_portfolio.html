About Me

Designing with purpose and precision

"Good design is invisible — it gets out of the way so the user can accomplish their goal."

Hi, I'm Drashti — a UI/UX Designer passionate about creating thoughtful digital experiences. I specialize in translating complex problems into clean, human-centered design solutions.

My approach blends research-driven insight with visual craft, ensuring every pixel serves a purpose. From wireframes to polished prototypes, I bring ideas to life with precision and care.

I enjoy collaborating closely with product teams and developers to ensure that great design doesn't stop at the mockup — it reaches the final product.

My Process

How I approach every design challenge

01
Discover

Deep dive into user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to understand the problem space thoroughly.

02
Define

Synthesize research into clear problem statements, user personas, and journey maps that guide every design decision.

03
Design

Rapid ideation through wireframes and hi-fi prototypes — always keeping user goals at the centre of every iteration.

04
Deliver

Usability testing, developer handoff with meticulous specs, and post-launch analysis to measure real-world impact.
